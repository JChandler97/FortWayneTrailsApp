In 2017 our community accomplished its 100th mile of paved trails in our network. In honor of that milestone, we've issued a challenge to the community to raise $100,000 for future trail development activities. As of April 6, 2018, over $88,000 has been donated. Help us celebrate this amazing achievement by participating in our 100 Mile Campaign.


Fort Wayne Trails, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Our mission is to act as a community partner and advocate with our municipal partners in the development of a connected, multipurpose system of trails in Allen County, Indiana.


Donation Policies:
Donations will be designated as general contributions to be used as leadership of Fort Wayne Trails decides unless restricted to a certain project by the donor. To restrict a donation, please indicate on the memo of the check, on the donation envelope provided by Fort Wayne Trails or include a note of instruction.


Restricted Donation Operational Support:
If the donation is restricted to a certain project, a 10% general operation fee will be assessed for each donation.
